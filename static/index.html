Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your questions about RetinaSeg.

RetinaSeg is an AI-powered web service that performs high-precision segmentation of blood vessels in retinal fundus images. It's designed to assist in the analysis of retinal health by providing clear visualizations of the vascular structure.

The service utilizes a state-of-the-art U-Net++ architecture with an EfficientNet-B4 backbone. The model was trained using a sophisticated 4-stage sequential training pipeline on public datasets like DRIVE and HRF.

You can upload images in standard formats such as JPG, PNG. The system is designed to handle high-resolution color images.
